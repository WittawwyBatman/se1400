Target Audience:

Geolocation - Where does the primary audience live?

The target audience primarily resides in urban fashion hubs such as New York, Los Angeles, Paris, and Tokyo.

Age - What are the primary age ranges?

Autrui caters to individuals aged 18-35, who appreciate luxury fashion and high-end streetwear.

Gender - Who is the majority audience?

The brand appeals to both men and women, with a slightly larger market share among young male fashion enthusiasts.

Other Demographics:

Our audience consists of trendsetters, influencers, creatives, and professionals who value exclusivity, style, and premium quality.
