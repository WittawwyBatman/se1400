Luxe Hoodie

Elevate your wardrobe with the Altruisme Luxe Hoodie, a premium heavyweight piece designed for those who appreciate high-end minimalism. This hoodie blends comfort and sophistication, making it an essential staple for any luxury streetwear collection.

Materials

Crafted from 100% organic heavyweight cotton, this hoodie provides unmatched durability and softness. The brushed interior ensures a cozy feel, while the structured fit maintains its shape wear after wear.

Design & Fit

Featuring an oversized, relaxed fit with dropped shoulders, the Luxe Hoodie offers an effortless silhouette. Ribbed cuffs and hem provide structure, while the deep, adjustable hood adds a modern edge.

Craftsmanship

Every detail is meticulously crafted, from the precision stitching to the reinforced seams, ensuring longevity and premium quality. The minimalist design is free of excessive branding, making it a timeless, versatile piece.

Versatility

Perfect for layering or wearing on its own, this hoodie pairs seamlessly with any outfit. Whether you're dressing up for a sleek urban look or keeping it casual, the Luxe Hoodie adapts to every style.

Fit Guide

  • Oversized fit for a modern, relaxed silhouette.
