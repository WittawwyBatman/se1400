Return & Refund Policy

We are committed to providing exceptional quality and ensuring customer satisfaction with every purchase. If you are not completely satisfied with your order, our return and refund policy is designed to make the process as smooth as possible.

Returns

  • Eligibility: Items must be returned within 30 days of receipt in their original condition, including all packaging, tags, and accessories. Items that have been worn, altered, washed, or damaged are not eligible for return.
  • Non-returnable items: Due to hygiene reasons, undergarments, swimwear, and custom-made pieces are not eligible for returns.
  • Proof of Purchase: A valid receipt or order confirmation is required for all returns.

Refunds

  • Processing Time: Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, the refund will be processed within 7-10 business days.
  • Shipping Costs: Original shipping costs are non-refundable. Return shipping costs will be deducted unless the return is due to a fault on our part (e.g., damaged or incorrect items).
  • Exchanges: We offer exchanges for defective or damaged items. Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order to initiate an exchange.

How to Return an Item

  1. Contact our customer service team at support@altruisme.com to initiate a return.
  2. We will provide you with a prepaid return label (if applicable) and instructions on how to ship the item back to us.
  3. Package the item securely in its original packaging and attach the return label.
