Who is Altruisme for?

Altruisme is designed for those who embrace high-end fashion and appreciate the finest craftsmanship. Our audience values exclusivity, luxury, and timeless style.

Target Demographics

  • Geolocation: Primarily North America and Europe, with growing markets in Asia and the Middle East.
  • Age Range: 20-45 years old, with a focus on young professionals and high-net-worth individuals.
  • Gender: Primarily gender-neutral, catering to those with refined fashion taste regardless of gender identity.
  • Other: Our customers are fashion-forward individuals who seek exclusivity, quality, and a brand that resonates with their luxurious lifestyle.
